About Me

Introvert, but loud when needed. That is a line that defines me. Hi! My name is Natasha Irene
Nunes and I come from the beautiful country of Oman. Even though I am an Indian by nationality,
I have spent the last 18 years of my life in Oman before moving to Canada.

I am a Second year Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo.
I am interested in a lot of things such as music, dance, photography, art and digital marketing but
the thing I love the most is fashion.

Fashion is an art form that allows you to express yourself and your views on a lot of issues.
I remember wearing different outfits, jewelry and shoes to school and not repeating my outfits more
than twice a month. Now that I have grown, I realize how much waste it produces and in turn how
much harm it causes to the environment. I believe fast fashion is one of the main causes of
environmental degradation and hence have decided to shop sustainably from thrift stores etc.

I am interested in working in the following fields; social media management, public relations, digital
marketing or CSR management in a fashion company.
